Towers of Hanoi is a game that consists of three rods and number of disks of different sizes which can slide into any rod.
Objective: Move the entire stack from one rod on to another (from to the to rod)
1. Only one disk can be moved at a time
2. No larger disk should be placed on top of a smaller disk
3. Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack/empty rod.
